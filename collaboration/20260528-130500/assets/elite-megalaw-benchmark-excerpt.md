## The satirical "methodology"

> The Elite MegaLaw Benchmark was developed internally using a proprietary enterprise benchmarking framework incorporating:
> - Recursive agentic orchestration
> - Multi-model legal cognition pathways
> - AI-native benchmark enhancement
> - Enterprise-grade chart smoothing
> - Strategic category weighting
> - Advanced decimal point optimisation

> Tasks were designed to simulate authentic large law firm conditions, including:
> - Reviewing documents moments before signing
> - Producing summaries no one reads
> - Turning simple workflows into platforms
> - Renaming existing features with the word agentic
> - Generating benchmark PDFs with dark blue gradients

> The report notes that all benchmarking was conducted independently by a highly qualified panel consisting entirely of MikeOSS.

## The tell (the real critique inside the joke)

The article's own closing names the failure mode it parodies: benchmark reports where "the sponsoring company ranks first in every category, all competitors cluster narrowly below, and the methodology section becomes progressively less understandable." That is the venom. The four `assay-pathways/` stubs are the proposed assay.
